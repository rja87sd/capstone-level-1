About Us

Looking For Gamers was founded by an avid tabletop gamer who recognized the need for a platform that goes beyond a simple matchmaking service. We are dedicated to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community, where everyone can find that perfect group of gaming companions to create memories that last a lifetime. Start your journey with LFG and discover a world of tabletop gaming possibilities. Whether you're a Game Master or Storyteller searching for a band of intrepid adventurers, or a solo player seeking a new party, our platform is designed to make your gaming experience richer and more enjoyable.

About Our Founder

Ryan Arnold is a tabletop gamer of 20 years. Growing up in small communities, he developed a love of fantasy novels and films that would set his imagination racing. Feeling the impact of COVID-19 on the gaming community, Ryan sought a way to help bring people back together at the gaming table. He found his solution when working on a class project to build a website. Thus, Looking For Gamers was born.
